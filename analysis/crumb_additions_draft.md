## Writing Style Notes

**Matching existing prose:**
- Use italics for internal thoughts (*Even when you should*)
- Short, punchy internal questions ("Could he?")
- Iwafune's self-recrimination ("orders were easier than conscience")
- Vague temporal references ("years", "that day", "the first time")
- Never name specific locations or give exact timeline
- Nagare's dialogue remains simple, childlike, but double-meaning
- Keep narrator voice consistent (third limited, Iwafune POV)

**What NOT to do:**
- ❌ Don't name the city
- ❌ Don't specify Iwafune's role (operative, soldier, etc.)
- ❌ Don't confirm Nagare recognized him at orphanage
- ❌ Don't explain the facility or organization
- ❌ Don't add more than these 5 moments (avoid over-explaining)
- ❌ Don't break the intimate tone with world-building details
