**Current Story:**
- Iwafune's love seems pure (adoptive father saving sick son)
- Guilt is about using parasite (crossing ethical line)

**Backstory Version:**
- Iwafune's love is ATONEMENT-BASED (caused Nagare's suffering)

### World-Building Conflicts:

**Current Story:**
- Minimal worldbuilding (outbreak, parasites, monsters)
- Facility/organization not mentioned
- Feels like global/random outbreak
- Iwafune is scientist with access to specimen

**Backstory:**
- Research facility as active antagonist
- Outbreak was CAUSED by facility (not natural)
- Iwafune has insider knowledge
- Infiltration mission (action/thriller elements)

**Impact**: Adds external threat, shifts genre toward conspiracy/action
